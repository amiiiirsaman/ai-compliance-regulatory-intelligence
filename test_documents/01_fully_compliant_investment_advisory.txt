IMPORTANT DISCLOSURES

This Investment Advisory Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between ABC Financial Advisors, LLC ("Adviser"), a Securities and Exchange Commission registered investment adviser, and the undersigned client ("Client").

1. ADVISORY SERVICES
The Adviser will provide investment advisory services including portfolio management, financial planning, and investment recommendations based on the Client's stated investment objectives, risk tolerance, and financial situation.

2. FIDUCIARY DUTY
ABC Financial Advisors acknowledges its fiduciary duty to act in the best interest of the Client at all times. We are committed to:
- Providing advice that is in your best interest
- Avoiding conflicts of interest or disclosing them fully
- Acting with the care, skill, prudence, and diligence of a prudent person
- Never placing our interests ahead of yours

3. FEE DISCLOSURE (FINRA Rule 2111 Compliant)
Annual Advisory Fee: 1.00% of assets under management
- Fee is calculated quarterly in arrears
- Fee is debited directly from your account
- No hidden fees, commissions, or 12b-1 fees
- You may terminate at any time and receive a pro-rated refund

4. RISK DISCLOSURE
All investments involve risk, including the potential loss of principal. Past performance does not guarantee future results. The value of your investments may fluctuate, and you may receive more or less than your original investment when you redeem your shares.

5. CONFLICTS OF INTEREST DISCLOSURE
We disclose the following potential conflicts of interest:
- We may recommend affiliated products where appropriate
- Our employees may invest in the same securities we recommend
- We receive compensation solely from client fees, not from product sales

6. PRIVACY NOTICE (Regulation S-P Compliant)
Your privacy is important to us. We collect and maintain personal information to provide our services. We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as permitted by law:
- With service providers who help us conduct our business
- As required by law or regulatory inquiry
- With your consent

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccuracies
- Opt-out of marketing communications

7. CLIENT SUITABILITY
Before making any recommendations, we will:
- Obtain information about your financial situation
- Understand your investment objectives
- Assess your risk tolerance
- Consider your investment time horizon
- Review your liquidity needs

8. COMPLAINT PROCEDURES
If you have a complaint about our services:
- Contact our Chief Compliance Officer at compliance@abcfinancial.com
- File a complaint with FINRA at www.finra.org
- File a complaint with the SEC at www.sec.gov

9. FORM ADV DISCLOSURE
You have received and acknowledge receipt of our Form ADV Part 2A (Firm Brochure) and Part 2B (Brochure Supplement). These documents contain important information about our qualifications, business practices, and conflicts of interest.

10. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with written notice. Upon termination, fees will be pro-rated to the date of termination.
