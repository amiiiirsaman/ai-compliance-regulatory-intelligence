3. PAYMENT FOR ORDER FLOW
We receive compensation from market makers for routing your orders to them. This creates a conflict of interest. In 2024, we received an average of $0.003 per share in PFOF. Despite this conflict, we believe our execution quality is competitive.

We may change requirements without notice and liquidate positions if maintenance falls short.
